📦 Supply Chain Analysis _ Delivery & Shipment Delay Insights

A data analytics project focused on identifying the root causes of shipment delays and delivery inefficiencies across a supply chain network, using Python for exploratory analysis, SQL for data querying, Power BI for visualization, and a written report to summarize findings and recommendations.

📌 Project Overview

Timely delivery is one of the most critical performance indicators in any supply chain. This project analyzes a supply chain dataset (sourced from Kaggle) to understand why shipments are delayed, which regions/suppliers/products are most affected, and what patterns emerge across order volume, shipping mode, and lead time , with the goal of supporting data-driven decisions to improve on-time delivery rates.

🎯 Objectives


Identify the key factors contributing to shipment and delivery delays
Analyze delay patterns across regions, suppliers, product categories, and shipping modes
Quantify the scale of the delay problem (e.g., % of late orders, average delay duration)
Visualize findings in an interactive dashboard for easy business consumption
Provide actionable recommendations to reduce delays

🔄 Project Workflow


Data Collection: Sourced supply chain dataset from Kaggle
Data Cleaning & EDA (Python) : Handled missing values and inconsistencies, explored delay distributions, and examined correlations between delay and variables like shipping mode, order priority, and distance
Querying & Aggregation (SQL) : Wrote queries to calculate delay rates by region, supplier, and product category, and to summarize average delay durations
Dashboard Development (Power BI) : Built an interactive dashboard visualizing on-time vs. delayed shipments, delay trends over time, and top contributing factors
Reporting (Word) – Compiled a full written report covering methodology, findings, and business recommendations
